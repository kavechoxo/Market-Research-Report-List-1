<p><strong>The Residential Humidifier Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vapor Type Humidifiers</li><li>Water Spray Humidifiers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The residential humidifier market features various types, primarily vapor type humidifiers and water spray humidifiers. Vapor type humidifiers generate steam by boiling water, releasing clean, warm moisture into the air, ideal for colder climates. Conversely, water spray humidifiers disperse a fine mist of water vapor without heating, providing a cooler alternative suitable for warmer environments. Both types aim to enhance indoor air quality, alleviating issues like dry skin and respiratory discomfort while creating a more comfortable living space.</p></p>
