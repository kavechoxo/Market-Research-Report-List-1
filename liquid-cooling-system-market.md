<p><p>The liquid cooling system market comprises three main types: Triple Radiator, Dual Radiator, and Single Radiator systems. The Triple Radiator system is designed for high-performance setups, efficiently managing heat from overclocked CPUs and GPUs. The Dual Radiator system provides a balanced cooling solution for medium to high-tier builds, ensuring good thermal management. The Single Radiator system is ideal for budget-conscious users or compact builds, offering basic cooling efficiency while maintaining a smaller physical footprint.</p></p>
