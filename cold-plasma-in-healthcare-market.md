The market is divided into two main types: low-pressure cold plasma and atmospheric cold plasma. Low-pressure cold plasma operates in vacuum conditions, ideal for sterilization and surface modifications, particularly in medical device production. In contrast, atmospheric cold plasma functions at ambient conditions, making it suitable for wound healing, infection control, and tissue regeneration, offering a versatile, non-invasive treatment option across various medical fields.</p></p>
