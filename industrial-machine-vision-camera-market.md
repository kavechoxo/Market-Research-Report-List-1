<p><strong>The Industrial Machine Vision Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Line Scan Camera</li><li>Area Scan Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial machine vision camera market primarily comprises two types: line scan cameras and area scan cameras. Line scan cameras capture images one line at a time, making them ideal for applications requiring high-speed inspection, such as in manufacturing conveyor systems. In contrast, area scan cameras capture complete images in a single frame, making them suitable for static inspections and 2D imaging tasks. Both camera types enhance automation and quality control across various industries by facilitating precise monitoring and analysis.</p></p>
