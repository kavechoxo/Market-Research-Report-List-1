<p><p>The spirometer mouthpiece market is categorized into two main types: disposable and reusable. Disposable mouthpieces are designed for single-use, minimizing cross-contamination and ensuring hygiene during pulmonary function tests. They are often preferred in clinical settings for their convenience. On the other hand, reusable mouthpieces are designed for multiple uses, typically made from durable materials that can be sterilized between uses.
