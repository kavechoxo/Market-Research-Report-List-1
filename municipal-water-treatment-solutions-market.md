<p><p>The Municipal Water Treatment Solutions market encompasses various technologies aimed at purifying water for public consumption. Reverse Osmosis Membranes effectively remove contaminants at a molecular level. Ion-exchange aids in softening water and removing specific ions. Ultrafiltration (UF) Membranes filter out larger particles and microorganisms. Electrodeionization (EDI) combines ion-exchange and electricity to produce high-purity water. Biological Filtration utilizes microbial activity to remove pollutants. Chemical Conditioning involves additives to improve water quality, while Clarification, Filtration, and Chlorination ensure safe drinking water by eliminating impurities and pathogens. Other technologies may include advanced oxidation processes and membrane bioreactors.</p></p>
