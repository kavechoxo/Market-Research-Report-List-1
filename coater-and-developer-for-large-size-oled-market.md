<p><p>In the large-size OLED market, coaters and developers play crucial roles in the manufacturing process. Coatters apply a uniform layer of organic materials onto substrates, essential for forming OLED layers. Developers, on the other hand, help in patterning the coated layers, selectively removing unwanted materials to define pixels and circuits. Together, these technologies optimize production efficiency and ensure high-quality display performance, catering to the growing demand for large-size OLED displays in televisions and digital signage.</p></p>
