<p><strong>The Wood Fillers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-Based</li><li>Water-Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wood fillers market is primarily divided into two types: solvent-based and water-based fillers. Solvent-based fillers offer strong adhesion and durability, making them ideal for high-traffic areas and outdoor applications. They dry quickly but may emit strong odors and require proper ventilation. In contrast, water-based fillers are more environmentally friendly, easy to clean up, and typically have lower VOC emissions. They are suitable for indoor use and provide a smooth finish, although they may take longer to dry compared to solvent-based options.</p></p>
