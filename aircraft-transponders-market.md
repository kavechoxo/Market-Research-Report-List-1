<p><ul><li>Mode S</li><li>Mode A</li><li>Mode C</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aircraft transponders market is categorized into three primary types: Mode A, Mode C, and Mode S. Mode A transponders transmit a simple identification code upon interrogation, primarily used in basic air traffic control environments. Mode C enhanced this by providing altitude information, improving aircraft tracking. Mode S offers further sophistication, transmitting both identification and altitude data, along with additional information, enabling selective interrogation by air traffic control. This tiered approach enhances situational awareness in increasingly crowded skies.</p></p>
