<p><p>Freelancer Management Systems (FMS) can be categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based FMS solutions offer flexibility, scalability, and ease of access, allowing users to manage freelancers remotely via the internet. This model often involves lower upfront costs and automatic updates. Both types aim to streamline freelancer engagement and project management.</p></p>
