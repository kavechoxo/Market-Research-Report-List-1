<p><strong>The Blind Flanges Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FF</li><li>RF</li><li>MFM</li><li>TG</li><li>RJ</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Blind flanges are sealing devices used in piping systems to close off ends and prevent fluid from escaping. The market features several types: Flat Face (FF) blind flanges lack raised surfaces, enabling direct contact with the surface of the pipe. Raised Face (RF) flanges have a protruding area, enhancing sealing and pressure tolerance. Modified Full Face (MFM) flanges combine features for improved sealing. Tongue and Groove (TG) flanges ensure a secure alignment, while Ring Joint (RJ) flanges utilize a metal ring for high-pressure applications.</p></p>
