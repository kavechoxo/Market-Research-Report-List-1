<p><p>The can coating additives market is primarily divided into two types: solventborne and waterborne. Solventborne additives utilize organic solvents, offering excellent adhesion and durability, making them suitable for challenging environments. In contrast, waterborne additives are eco-friendly, containing lower volatile organic compounds (VOCs), and are increasingly favored due to regulatory pressures and environmental concerns. Both types are integral to enhancing the performance of coatings, influencing attributes such as flexibility, corrosion resistance, and overall finish quality in metal can applications.</p></p>
