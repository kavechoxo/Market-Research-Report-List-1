<p><strong>The Greywater System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gravity-Based Systems</li><li>Pumped Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The greywater system market is primarily categorized into gravity-based and pumped systems. Gravity-based systems utilize natural gravitational forces to transport and treat greywater, making them low-maintenance and cost-effective. In contrast, pumped systems rely on mechanical pumps to move greywater, allowing for greater flexibility in installation but requiring more maintenance and energy. Both types cater to diverse applications, from residential to commercial, helping to promote sustainable water management practices by recycling water for irrigation and non-potable uses.</p></p>
