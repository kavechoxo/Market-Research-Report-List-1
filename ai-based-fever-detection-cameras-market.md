<p><p>AI-based fever detection cameras are categorized into three types based on their wavelength sensitivity: short-wave, mid-wave, and long-wave length cameras. Short-wave length cameras primarily detect visible thermal radiation, offering high-resolution images for accurate temperature measurement. Mid-wave length cameras are effective for capturing heat signatures, ideal for detecting fevers in various environments. Long-wave length cameras, typically used for thermal imaging, excel in low-light conditions, providing reliable fever detection even from a distance. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing public health safety.</p></p>
