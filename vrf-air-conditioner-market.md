<p><p>The VRF air conditioner market is categorized primarily into horizontal and vertical branch pipe types. Horizontal branch pipes are designed for installations where space constraints or architectural designs necessitate a flat layout, facilitating efficient airflow distribution across multiple indoor units. In contrast, vertical branch pipes are optimal for tall buildings, allowing for streamlined vertical installation, maximizing efficiency, and catering to various floor layouts. Both types ensure flexible and efficient cooling solutions in diverse commercial and residential environments.</p></p>
