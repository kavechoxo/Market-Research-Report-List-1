<p><p>The Buckytube market encompasses two primary types: single-walled and multi-walled carbon nanotubes. Single-walled carbon nanotubes (SWCNTs) feature a single layer of carbon atoms arranged in a cylindrical shape, known for their incredible strength and electrical properties, making them suitable for electronics and materials applications. On the other hand, multi-walled carbon nanotubes (MWCNTs) consist of multiple concentric layers of carbon tubes, offering enhanced structural stability and larger surface areas, ideal for uses in composites, energy storage, and sensors.</p></p>
