<p><p>The Desiccant Caps and Canisters market comprises two primary types: Desiccant Caps and Desiccant Canisters. Desiccant Caps are integrated directly into product packaging, providing moisture control within a sealed environment, ideal for smaller items like pharmaceuticals and food products. Desiccant Canisters, on the other hand, are standalone units that can be placed in various containers or storage spaces, offering flexible moisture management for larger volumes, including electronics and industrial applications. Both types enhance product longevity by preventing moisture-related degradation.</p></p>
