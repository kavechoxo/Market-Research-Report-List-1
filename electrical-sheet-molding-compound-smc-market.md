<p><p>Electrical Sheet Molding Compound (SMC) is a thermosetting material used for electrical applications due to its excellent insulating properties and heat resistance. The market is primarily segmented into two types: polyester-based SMC and vinyl ester-based SMC. Polyester-based SMC offers good mechanical strength and chemical resistance, making it suitable for general electrical applications. In contrast, vinyl ester-based SMC provides superior resistance to corrosion and heat, making it ideal for high-performance environments, such as electrical enclosures and components in harsh conditions.</p></p>
