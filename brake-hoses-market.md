Nylon hoses offer lightweight alternatives with improved resistance to abrasion and chemicals. Metal brake hoses, typically stainless steel, provide enhanced strength and minimize pressure loss, making them suitable for high-performance vehicles. PTFE hoses are known for their excellent chemical resistance and temperature stability, ideal for specialized applications.</p></p>
