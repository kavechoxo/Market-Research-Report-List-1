<p><p>The HPV diagnostic product market includes several key types: In Situ Hybridization (ISH) detects HPV DNA directly in tissue samples, providing precise localization of infection. HC2 Technology, or Hybrid Capture 2, uses nucleic acid hybridization to identify high-risk HPV types, offering a sensitive screening method. Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) is a powerful technique that amplifies HPV DNA for detection, allowing for high specificity and sensitivity. These methods collectively enhance cervical cancer screening and management, catering to diverse diagnostic needs.</p></p>
