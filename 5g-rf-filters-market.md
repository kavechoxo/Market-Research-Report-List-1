<p><strong>The 5G RF Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SAW Filter</li><li>BAW Filter</li><li>FBAR Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 5G RF filters market encompasses several key types: SAW (Surface Acoustic Wave) filters, BAW (Bulk Acoustic Wave) filters, and FBAR (Film Bulk Acoustic Resonator) filters. SAW filters are widely used for their compact size and low cost, ideal for low-frequency applications. BAW filters provide superior performance at higher frequencies, offering better selectivity and efficiency. FBAR filters, a subcategory of BAW, use thin-film technology for enhanced miniaturization and power handling, making them suitable for high-performance 5G applications.</p></p>
