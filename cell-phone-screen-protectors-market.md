<p><p>The cell phone screen protector market consists of three main types: TG (Tempered Glass) screen protectors, PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) film, and AR (Anti-Reflective) protectors. TG screen protectors offer high durability and scratch resistance, providing strong protection against impacts. PET film is a thinner, flexible option that provides basic protection against scratches, while maintaining clarity. AR protectors reduce glare and reflections, enhancing visibility in bright conditions. Each type caters to different user preferences and protection needs.</p></p>
