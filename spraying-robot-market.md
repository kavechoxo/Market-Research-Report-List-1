Programming Input Type robots are pre-programmed with specific tasks and routes, allowing for high precision in repetitive tasks. In contrast, Teaching Input Type robots learn through demonstration; operators manually guide the robot through desired movements, enabling adaptability to various tasks without extensive programming. Both types cater to different operational needs in industries such as agriculture, manufacturing, and painting.</p></p>
