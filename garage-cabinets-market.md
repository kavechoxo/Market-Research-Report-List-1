<p><p>Garage cabinets come in two primary types: freestanding and wall-mounted. Freestanding cabinets are versatile, allowing for easy relocation and organization of tools and supplies without drilling into walls. They often feature a sturdy design for heavy items. In contrast, wall-mounted cabinets optimize floor space and create a more organized look by utilizing vertical storage. They are securely attached to walls, offering durability and accessibility, making them ideal for smaller garages where floor space is limited.</p></p>
