<p><p>Smart Set-Top Boxes (STBs) facilitate the reception and decoding of digital television signals. The market is segmented into various types: </p><p>1. **Digital Cable STBs** connect to cable services, enabling access to numerous channels.</p><p>2. **Satellite Digital STBs** receive signals from satellites for wider coverage.</p><p>3. **Terrestrial Digital STBs** decode signals transmitted over the air.</p><p>4. **IPTV STBs** stream content via internet protocols, offering on-demand services.</p><p>5. **Others** include hybrid STBs that combine functionalities or cater to specialized services.</p></p>
