<p><p>The commercial vehicle brake controls market encompasses systems like Anti-lock Braking System (ABS) and Electronic Stability Control (ESC). ABS prevents wheel lock-up during braking, enhancing vehicle control and safety on slippery surfaces. ESC further improves stability by detecting loss of traction and adjusting brake force on individual wheels, helping to prevent skidding. Together, these technologies significantly enhance vehicle safety, reduce accident rates, and comply with increasingly stringent regulatory standards, making them essential for modern commercial vehicles.</p></p>
