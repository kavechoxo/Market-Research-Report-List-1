<p><p>The high-pressure misting pumps market consists of two primary types: pulley pumps and direct pumps. Direct pumps, on the other hand, are designed for direct connection to the motor, offering compactness and straightforward installation. Both types cater to agricultural, cooling, and insect control needs, each with distinct advantages based on application requirements.</p></p>
