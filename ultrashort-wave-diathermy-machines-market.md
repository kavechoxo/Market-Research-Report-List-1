<p><p>Ultrashort wave diathermy machines encompass two primary types: short wave diathermy and pulsed short wave diathermy. Short wave diathermy utilizes high-frequency electromagnetic waves to generate deep tissue heat, promoting healing and pain relief in various musculoskeletal conditions. In contrast, pulsed short wave diathermy delivers energy in short bursts, effectively reducing tissue temperature and minimizing discomfort while still promoting therapeutic effects. Both techniques are widely used in physical therapy, rehabilitation, and pain management, catering to diverse patient needs.</p></p>
